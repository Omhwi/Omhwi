Frequently Asked Questions

How do I **search for a venue by theme**?

You have a few easy options! First, visit the **Party Themes** page, where you can browse or filter venues using predefined tags like 'Elegant,' 'Rustic,' 'Teen,' or 'Tropical.' Alternatively, use the **Quick Search bar** at the top of any page to type in specific keywords (e.g., "Roaring Twenties," "Rooftop View," or "Large Capacity").

What is Omhwi's **cancellation policy**?

Omhwi acts as a booking platform, and cancellation policies are **set directly by each individual venue**. You can find the specific policy (e.g., 'Strict,' 'Moderate,' or 'Flexible') on the venue's listing page, usually under the **'Policies'** tab or in the booking summary before you confirm payment. Please review this carefully, as deposit refunds depend entirely on the venue's stated terms.

How can I **contact a venue before booking**?

We encourage communication! On every venue's detailed listing page, you'll find a **'Contact Host'** or **'Inquire Now'** button. Clicking this opens our secure, internal messaging system, allowing you to ask the venue operator questions about availability, capacity, special requests, or pricing details before you make a reservation.

Do you offer **custom theme packages**?

Absolutely! If our curated themes don't quite match your vision, use the **Request Custom Birthday Venue** section at the bottom of the 'Party Themes' page. Our dedicated **Venue Specialists** will review your request and personally match you with the perfect venue and necessary vendors to bring your unique party idea to life.

I have an urgent issue. How can I **speak to a person**?

For immediate assistance with existing bookings or technical issues, please use the **Chat Live with Support** button in the section below. This connects you directly with a member of our customer support team during operating hours. For less urgent inquiries, you can send an email to support@omhwi.com.

Need to speak to a person?

Our dedicated support team is available to assist you with urgent issues, booking problems, or specific questions that aren't answered above.
